## About
Lyra Chic is a website that provide the content about music, art, and mutual relationship with editorial-look design and serif font, thus making customers feel comfortable.

Using the Lyra UI micro-framework, Lyra Chic delivers an elegant experience with consistent editorial serif design, a clear purpose, and a well-articulated layout.

## Key Features
1. Responsive design powered by __Lyra UI__.
2. Smooth scrolling experience with custom parallax and Lenis.
3. Introducing  PeopleCard with People Panel (side-panel view)
4. __Build Page__ — let you to explore our system in Web Design, music, and art.
5. 7 active members with their in-depth stories.
6. __Lyra DIY__ — Advanced UI Advanced UI with a touch of creativity that offers color palette tones, comet effect, glare animation, and better font consistency.
7. __Lyra AI__ — Make charm by asking the product and website features with a soft heart.
8. __Project Diana__ — Explore our hidden features or Labs that are still in development by holding the Talk to Lyra floating button for 3 seconds. This includes: __Show member zodiac__, __editable section title__, __comet animation__, __PeopleCard with 3 different styles__, and many more!
9. __Your Cart (ALPHA)__ – Add your favorite music or art to your wishlist with price. No gimmick, no sign-in required (some personal information are needed for transaction).

## What is this foundation build? 
This website is build upon vanilla CSS and JavaScript (with ECMAScript 6 and TypeScript for modules). In addition, we also compiled this website either to Vercel and GitHub Pages.
