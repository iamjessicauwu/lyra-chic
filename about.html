An Editorial Introduction to Lyra Chic

Lyra Chic is an editorial-like agency that focused on creative art, music, and mutual relationship.

Keyza Richi as a founder of Lyra

Keyza Richi and Yuuki Conan at the same high school were thinking of ways to make Richi popular thanks to her interior design and fashion, which are aesthetic, precise, yet practical for everyone. Their idea to build Lyra required a great deal of effort, as Yuuki was the sole initial developer of this website.
After a few months, Keisya Ayu joined the team to make the website accessible to international visitors, with flexible language localization features. Today, Lyra is the leading website for finding and recommending interior design, fashion, and graphic design ideas in Indonesia.

The "Chic" refers to the refresh of the Lyra

After 1 year of Lyra Aura development, we are excited to introduce the refreshed version of our beloved platform, Lyra Chic. Nathania Anneta, who has been a key contributor to the project, has played a vital role in shaping the new features and improvements. Though not perfect, but we believe it's a step in the right direction.

In May 2026, while Jessica Noleen returning from a busy day of work, she promoted Lyra Chic at her old high school. There, she recruited her classmate named Carin Miracle, as a new member. Within a few weeks, the team expanded to include more talented individuals, such as Monique, Mae, and Charis. They play important roles in the continued success of Lyra Chic, especially their role in music and arts.
